# NordicCo Synthetic Credit Agreement Extract

Document ID: CA-2025
Synthetic covenant language for demo purposes only.

## Definitions

**Consolidated EBITDA** means EBITDA as reported in the quarterly compliance certificate, adjusted only for documented non-recurring items approved by the lender.

**Net Debt** means senior debt outstanding minus unrestricted cash.

**Liquidity** means unrestricted cash plus undrawn committed revolving credit facilities.

## Financial covenants

### Net leverage covenant
The borrower must not permit Net Debt divided by Consolidated EBITDA to exceed **4.00x** on any quarterly test date.

### Interest coverage covenant
The borrower must maintain Consolidated EBITDA divided by cash interest expense of at least **2.50x** on any quarterly test date.

### Minimum liquidity covenant
The borrower must maintain Liquidity of at least **EUR 15.0 million** on any quarterly test date.

## Reporting

The borrower must deliver a quarterly compliance certificate within 45 days of each quarter end. The certificate must include EBITDA, senior debt, unrestricted cash, cash interest expense, liquidity and covenant calculations.
